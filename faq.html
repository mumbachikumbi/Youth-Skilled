Frequently Asked Questions

How does Youth Skilled work?

Users learn a skill, take an assessment to get verified and then choose to showcase their services. Clients can then find and hire them based on their skills, reviews and location.

Who is Youth Skilled for?

Youth Skilled is designed for:

  • People who want to learn practical, income-generating skills.
  • Skilled individuals who want to showcase their services.
  • Clients and businesses looking to hire trusted, verified talent.

It is designed to be used every day by anyone involved in skills development and employment.

What do the badges mean?

🔵 Blue YS Badge

Awarded to users who complete a Youth Skilled course, pass the assessment and choose to list themselves as service providers.

🟢 Green YS Badge

Awarded to skilled individuals who did not take a Youth Skilled course but passed a competency assessment to verify their skills before offering services.

Both badges confirm that a person's skills have been verified.

What skills or services are available?

Youth Skilled supports a wide range of practical and digital skills, including:

  • Tailoring and Fashion Design
  • Hair Braiding and Beauty Services
  • Baking and Catering
  • Graphic Design
  • Web and App Development
  • Photography and Videography
  • Mobile Phone Repair
  • Digital Marketing and Social Media Management

Some of these skills are available as Youth Skilled courses, while others may only be offered by verified service providers.

This allows clients to discover and hire trusted skilled individuals, even when a particular skill is not offered as a course on the platform.
